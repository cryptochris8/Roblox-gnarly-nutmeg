# Playtest Day — the family test script

A ~25-minute script for the first real multi-human session (Chris + the girls,
two or more devices). This is the first time two humans share a server, so the
goal is half "is it fun?", half "does multiplayer hold up?". Take notes on
anything that feels off — feel notes are as valuable as bug notes.

## Setup (2 min)

1. Chris: open the published place, **Servers tab → Start a private server**
   (or just have everyone join the same public server if it's public).
2. Everyone joins. At least one player on a **phone or tablet** — mobile is the
   #1 thing to validate.
3. Sanity check: everyone sees the same score on the jumbotrons, and the
   announcer says "Game start!" at kickoff.

## Part 1 — Free play (5 min)

Just play an exhibition together. Watch for:

- [ ] Ball rolls IN FRONT of whoever is dribbling — on their own screen AND
      on everyone else's (this was rebuilt; it should feel glued like FIFA).
- [ ] Passing between two humans feels crisp; the reception assist sticks.
- [ ] One human passes to a BOT, keeps sprinting, and gets the one-two back.
- [ ] Tackles/kicks show real leg swings; keeper dives on hard shots.
- [ ] Goal moment: horn + roar + announcer + replay camera + celebration —
      does it land? Does it ever feel too long?

## Part 2 — Host a family cup (10 min)

1. Anyone presses **🏆 NUTMEG TROPHY** and picks a nation.
2. Everyone else's picker pops with the countdown — **each player claims a
   different nation** within 20 seconds.
3. Play the bracket out.

- [ ] Both/all humans end up on THEIR claimed nation's side when their
      fixtures come up (check the kit colour + scoreboard name).
- [ ] When two claimed nations meet, the humans are on OPPOSITE teams.
- [ ] Eliminated players keep playing as neutrals without weirdness.
- [ ] The champion gets the trophy ceremony; Trophies stat ticks up.
- [ ] Bracket board between rounds reads correctly.

## Part 3 — Mobile deep-dive (5 min, on the phone)

- [ ] All 8 touch buttons reachable with thumbs without covering the view?
      (Shoot big near the right thumb, skills in the small top row.)
- [ ] Hold-to-charge shooting works by holding the Shoot button.
- [ ] Nation picker, quest panel, career panel all fit on screen.
- [ ] Frame rate playable in the busiest moment (goal + confetti + replay).

## Part 4 — Break it (3 min)

- [ ] One player leaves mid-cup — does their nation keep playing as bots?
- [ ] Two players claim the same nation — second one gets told no?
- [ ] Spam skill keys with no ball / during kickoff freeze — nothing breaks?
- [ ] Stand AFK — bots still chase and play around you?

## Capture

Note: device, what felt great, what felt bad, any error toasts, and the one
thing each player would change. Screenshots/recordings of anything weird.
